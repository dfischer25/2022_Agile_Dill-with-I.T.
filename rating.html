Pickelball Rating

Pickleball ratings are used to determine your skill level in pickleball. The ratings range from 1.0 to 5.0 by 0.5 increments. Enter your skill level with the different important skills required for pickleball and find our your pickleball rating. Pickleball ratings are used for tournaments to divide up brackets and also for recreational play to find people to play with who are of a similiar skill level.
